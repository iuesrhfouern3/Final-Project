Attractions in Europe and Austrailia

One of the greatest natural wonders in Europe are the Faroe Islands. These islands, located off the coast of Denmark, are a self driven country that is in the Kingdom of Denmark. Although this island is near the North Pole, tempuratures are above freezing due to the Gulf Stream. The island's name in Danish means, "the islands of sheep." The Faroe islands also compete in certain sports with their own national team.

Another one of most beautiful places you could visit in Europe is the Plitvice Lakes National Park in Croatia. This place has 16 lakes linked together by streams and waterfalls. Everything comes together like jewels and it seems as if every turn is and introduction to a new shade of blue or green. The lakes were founded in 1949 and they are in the largest national park in Croatia. The lakes are formed from pools and holes in the mountainsides. Now, going into Austrailia, we can visit the greatest wonder in Austrailia which is the Great Barrier Reef. The Great Barrier Reef stretches for about 3000 kilometers, and at it's widest stretches to about 65 kilometers. It has a collection of about 400 differnt types of coral and also features over 1500 species of fish, 200 species of birds, and around 20 types of reptiles. The reef is also home to clams, some at over 120 years old. This remarkable place is also home to some endangered speciec such as the large Green Sea Turtle. At over 500,000 years old, the Great Barrier Reef is also a migration spot for whales coming from the Antarctic.
