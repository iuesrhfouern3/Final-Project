Attractions in North and South America

There are many attracions in North and South America. To begin, let's start in South America. One of the places you should visit is The River of Five Colours in Colombia. This place is colorful and includes different types of plants. Besides having all of this, there are also no fish or other creatures so you could swim in the water. This place is most colorful in the months of July through November.

Another place worth visiting in South America is Angel falls in Venezuala. This extraordinary waterfall falls at 979 meters from the top, but the longest uninterrupted drop is 807 meters. The falls come from the Carro river and fall off near the submit of the Auyan-tepu mountain. The falls are located in the Guayana highlands, the second biggest national park in Venezuela and sixth in the world. The mountain was named after Jimmy Angel who, with his parter, flew and saw the falls in 1933. He then returned with his friends and wife and, when they traveled to the bottom after their plane got stuck, got the falls named after him.
